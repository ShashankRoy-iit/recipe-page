Thai Curry with Quinoa Recipe

Thai Curry with Quinoa
jump to ingredients
jump to procedure

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Cooking Time: 30 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Planning to host a house party or a brunch with family and friends, but confused about ‘what to cook’? Then fret not, we have got your back with this easy and delicious Thai Curry served with healthy pan-tossed Quinoa.
So, follow us through these easy steps, and enjoy this delightful vegan meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, broccoli)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Rinse the quinoa under cold water and cook it according to the package instructions. Set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the red curry paste and cook for 2 minutes to release its flavors.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and vegetable broth, and bring to a simmer.
  6. Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve the curry over the cooked quinoa and garnish with fresh cilantro.

Pasta

Pasta

Indulge in the rich and creamy flavors of our delectable pasta recipe, perfect for a comforting meal. This dish combines al dente pasta with a luscious sauce made from fresh ingredients, creating a harmonious blend of taste and texture. Whether you're a seasoned chef or a beginner in the kitchen, this recipe is designed to be easy to follow, ensuring that you can enjoy a restaurant-quality meal right at home.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the halved cherry tomatoes to the skillet and cook until they start to soften, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  5. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until it melts into the sauce. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet and toss to coat it evenly with the sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil leaves before serving.

Greek Salad

Greek Salad

A refreshing salad with cucumbers, tomatoes, olives, and feta cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup olives
  • 100g feta cheese
  • Olive oil & oregano

Procedure:

  1. Mix cucumber, tomatoes, and olives in a bowl.
  2. Top with crumbled feta cheese.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and sprinkle oregano.
  4. Toss gently and serve chilled.

Tomato Basil Soup

Tomato Basil Soup

A warm and comforting soup perfect for cold evenings.

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • Fresh basil leaves

Procedure:

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in a pot.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Pour in vegetable broth and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Blend until smooth, add basil, and serve hot.

Chocolate Cake

Chocolate Cake

A rich and moist chocolate cake for dessert lovers.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk

Procedure:

  1. Mix flour, cocoa, and sugar in a bowl.
  2. Add eggs and milk, whisk until smooth.
  3. Pour into a greased baking tin.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.

Vegetable Curry

Vegetable Curry

A flavorful curry made with mixed vegetables and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 cup peas
  • 2 onions
  • Spices (turmeric, cumin, coriander)

Procedure:

  1. Sauté onions in oil until golden.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and spices.
  3. Pour in water and simmer until vegetables are tender.
  4. Serve hot with rice or bread.
